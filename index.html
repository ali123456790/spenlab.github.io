About Spenn Lab

Spenn Lab is an interdisciplinary research group dedicated to advancing cutting-edge technologies in cybersecurity, privacy, and networked systems. We develop novel methods for secure data sharing, enhance IoT security, and forge collaborations that drive real-world impact.

  • Develop secure data-sharing techniques
  • Improve privacy algorithms for autonomous vehicles (AVs)
  • Strengthen IoT and smart grids against cyber threats
  • Collaborate with industry for real-world applications
