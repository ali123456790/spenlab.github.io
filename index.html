About Spen Lab

The Security and Privacy of Emerging Networks (SPEN) Research Lab is an interdisciplinary research group dedicated to advancing cutting-edge technologies in cybersecurity, privacy, and networked systems. We develop novel methods for secure data sharing, enhance IoT security, and forge collaborations that drive real-world impact.

  • Privacy-preserving techniques for autonomous vehicles and electric vehicles charging
  • Hardware-based security solutions for Internet of Things (IoT) applications
  • Federated learning and AI-based intrusion detection systems
  • Blockchain-based energy trading for smart grid networks
  • Privacy-aware authentication schemes using physical unclonable functions

We believe in fostering an environment of innovation, mentorship, and collaboration. Our work spans fundamental theoretical research and practical, applied solutions with high societal impact.

About Our Lead Academic Supervisor

Dr. Ahmed B. T. Sherif, Senior Member, IEEE

Associate Professor, School of Computing Sciences & Computer Engineering,
